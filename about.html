About Our Organization

Learn about our history, mission, and the values that guide our community since 1995

Our History

Shri Mumbai Purush Samaj was established in 1995 by a group of visionary individuals who recognized the need for a platform where men could connect, grow, and contribute to society.

What began as a small gathering of 15 individuals in a local community hall has grown into a respected organization with over 500 active members, multiple community initiatives, and a strong network of professionals and entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

To foster a supportive community that promotes:

  • Cultural preservation and traditional values
  • Professional and personal development
  • Social responsibility and community service
  • Leadership development and mentorship
  • Networking and collaborative opportunities

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Integrity

We conduct all activities with honesty, transparency, and the highest ethical standards. Our decisions are always made in the best interest of our community.

Community

We believe in the power of collective growth and mutual support. Every member is valued and contributes to our shared success.

Service

We are committed to serving our members and the wider community through meaningful initiatives and social responsibility programs.

Growth

We foster an environment of continuous learning and development, helping members achieve personal and professional excellence.
